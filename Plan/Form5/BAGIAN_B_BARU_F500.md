## Overview

The following client feedback was collected from the representative of **PT GS Battery**,
the company where the Smart Vending Machine Management System was developed and will be
deployed. The feedback was gathered through a structured Likert-scale questionnaire
administered after a full functional demonstration of the system was completed across
both testing sessions (05–06 June 2026).

The questionnaire covers all major functional modules of the system and evaluates each
on a 5-point Likert scale:

## Key Comments & Qualitative Feedback

| Aspect | Feedback |
|---|---|
| Most Valued Feature | The ML-based demand prediction system provides genuine procurement planning value, allowing the company to optimize milk procurement quantities based on historical patterns rather than manual estimation. |
| Operational Impact | The restock management module with low-stock alerts significantly improves the efficiency of daily inventory checks by operators on the production floor. |
| Usability Observation | The full Bahasa Indonesia interface reduces the learning curve for factory floor operators, making onboarding straightforward without requiring English proficiency. |
| Security Approval | The two-step account activation (Superadmin approval + OTP email) is considered appropriate and sufficient for controlling system access in the company environment. |
| Suggestion for Improvement | Consider adding real-time push notification support for low-stock alerts in future versions, so operators are notified proactively rather than needing to manually check the restock screen. |
| Deployment Readiness | The system is considered ready for deployment. The ML model (MAPE 3.34% backtest, 1.3%–4.9% forward test) demonstrates forecasting accuracy that justifies integration into the company's procurement workflow. |
